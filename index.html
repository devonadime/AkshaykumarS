About

Greetings to everyone, Mr. AKSHAY KUMAR S is an undergraduate student. He is currently studying engineering course, pursuing in mechanical stream; second year in R V INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND MANAGEMENT. He is an Engineer to be in couple of years. He is from Bengaluru city, Karnataka state. He is always been a keen learner and a competent professional. In any field, his efforts are sincere, meticulous and result oriented . He doesn't have a specific area of interest, you can call him as an allrounder. Whatever work he does , he gives one hundred percent and does it to the fullest with a lot of dedication and determination. He is a hard worker as well as a smart worker. He is an occasional singer, good orator and a writer, loves watching and playing cricket and football, and every other sport. He is socially connected and responsible, doesn't get setbacked in any situation. He always follows and has inculcated a line :"DHAIRYAM SARVATHRA SAADHANAM" (Courage is tool everywhere).
